## Delete Connections

You may delete any connection at any time.

To delete a connection, tap the **Institution** icon to open the history screen.

In the upper-left corner there is a trash can icon. When you tap it a confirmation dialog is displayed. If you want to delete the connection, tap **Delete**.Â If you don't, you can click **Cancel**.
